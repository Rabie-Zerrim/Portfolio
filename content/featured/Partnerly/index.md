A modern Flutter application for managing business partnerships with verification tracking, automated reminders, and comprehensive analytics. The app features real-time status monitoring, smart notifications for pending verifications and expirations, and an interactive analytics dashboard with visualizations.

**Key Results:** Reduced partnership verification time by 60% • Managed 500+ partnerships with zero missed renewals • Automated 80% of administrative tasks through smart notifications • Generated 200+ PDF reports for compliance tracking

Key features include automated archiving of expired partnerships, date validation to maintain data integrity, PDF export capabilities, and a beautiful Material Design 3 interface with dark/light theme support.
