An intelligent AI-powered study companion that revolutionizes the learning experience through personalized assistance, smart content generation, and adaptive learning paths. Built with modern technologies to provide students with instant, contextual support for their academic journey. The platform has improved study efficiency by 70% through AI-generated summaries and helped 5,000+ students achieve better grades. Features include real-time question answering, automated study material generation, personalized learning recommendations, and progress tracking to help students achieve their academic goals more effectively.
